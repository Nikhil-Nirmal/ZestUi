3.  Badge :

Badges are small circles, positioned either at top-right or bottom-right of the parent component.

Badge can be used to display numbers, online / offline status, depending on where they are used.

Some examples are shown below:

                
    <div class="badge">
        <img src="../../Images/avatar1.jpg" class="avatar-lg" alt="">
        <div class="on"></div>
    </div>

    <div class="badge">
        <img src="../../Images/avatar2.png" class="avatar-lg" alt="">
        <div class="off"></div>
    </div>

    <div class="badge">
        <img src="../../Images/avatar3.jpg" class="avatar-lg" alt="">
        <div class="dnd"></div>
    </div>
